The navigation bar

These commonly used functions can be found in the handy navigation bar at the bottom of your screen.

  • Home: Tap this to return to your skills palette at any time.

  • Event schedule: The full Grow Your Way agenda, at your fingertips.

  • Scan: Use this function to scan QR codes at event activities, Skills Showcases or talks.

  • Refresh: Progress not reflecting correctly? Tap once to retrieve an up-to-date status.

  • Your QR: To claim prizes you’ve earned, present this QR code at redemption booths.
